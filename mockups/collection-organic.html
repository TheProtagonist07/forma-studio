Geometry that grew here.

Vessels and planters whose forms are borrowed from coral, seed pods, and river-worn stone — generated by algorithms that imitate growth, then resolved by hand until they feel inevitable.

The Intention

Nothing in nature is extruded. Surfaces swell, taper, and fold because forces moved through them — water, wind, the slow push of a seed. Terra asks the printer to behave less like a machine and more like weather.

Specimen 01 — Vessel

Seed Vault

A vase ribbed like a drying seed pod. The ribs are generated by a script simulating differential growth, then thinned where fingers naturally land. No two production runs use the same seed value — each batch is its own harvest.
