The Philosophy

This is not a store. It is a record of intention — every curve argued over, every wall thickness chosen, every failure kept on display.

01 / INTENT

Designed, not downloaded

Every object begins as a sketch and a question. The geometry is original — modeled from zero in parametric CAD, owned end to end, refined until the form earns its existence.

02 / CRAFT

Grown layer by layer

Manufactured in-studio on machines we calibrate ourselves. Tolerances measured, surfaces finished by hand, every unit inspected against a checklist before it leaves.

03 / CODE

Software is the material

A self-built pipeline turns your name, your dimensions, your idea into a one-of-one object — parametric models, automated quoting, a factory that fits on a desk.

The Studio

A factory the size of a desk, run by software.

FORMA is a one-designer studio in Lucknow. Industrial design, mechanical engineering, and a self-hosted software stack share the same bench — CAD models flow through an automated pipeline into print queues, quality checks, and your hands. Small by choice. Precise by obsession.
